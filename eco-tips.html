🌱 Eco-Friendly Tips

Simple actions that make a big impact

🏠 Home

  • Switch off lights when not in use
  • Use reusable shopping bags
  • Compost food waste

🎓 School

  • Bring a reusable water bottle
  • Use both sides of paper
  • Recycle old notebooks

💼 Office

  • Turn off your monitor after work
  • Digitize documents instead of printing
  • Use reusable coffee mugs

🚲 Travel

  • Bike or walk for short distances
  • Carpool with colleagues
  • Use public transport more often
